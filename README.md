# AI Is in the Fingertips

**A 15-Video Industry Awareness and Product Thinking Program**

> Audience: Engineering Students (CS / IT) | Format: Recorded Video | Duration per Video: 30–45 Minutes

---

## Course Philosophy

This is not a coding course. It is designed for engineering students who want to develop a confident, well-informed understanding of Artificial Intelligence as it exists in the real world — the products being built, the companies building them, the careers being shaped, and the ideas driving it all.

The course is built around two outcomes: **product mindset** and **career awareness**. Every session is anchored in real companies, real products, and real decisions.

## How to Use This Repository

- Click a class link above to open its page.
- Each class page has the **video recording link** and a **reflection question**.
- Open `notes.md` inside each class folder for detailed revision notes.
- Submit your reflection response via the LMS after each class.

## Assessment

- No coding assignments or technical tests.
- Assessment is based on video completion, reflection responses, and a **final one-page personal AI roadmap**.
- Top submissions will be recognised and shared with your college.
